Our Story: The Heart Behind The Crafty Naari

At The Crafty Naari, we believe that every handmade piece carries a soul, a story, and a spark of joy. Based in the culturally rich city of Kolkata, India, our small but passionate brand is dedicated to creating handcrafted treasures that blend traditional Indian aesthetics with contemporary charm.

From hand-painted brooches and fridge magnets to coasters, wall hangings, and more – our creations are inspired by everyday life, nostalgia, and the vibrant colors of Indian culture. Each item is lovingly crafted to add a personal touch to your space, wherever in the world you may be.

We’ve had the honour of participating in several exhibition-cum-sale events, where we’ve connected with wonderful people, shared our craft, and grown with every experience. These moments continue to inspire us to stay true to our roots while embracing creativity with an open heart.

The Crafty Naari is not just a brand; it's a journey of love, art, and everything handmade.
